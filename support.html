Support

Noosh for iOS · San Soro Studio Inc.

We are here to help with Noosh, the recipe manager and pantry app for iPhone and iPad. For questions, feedback, billing or subscription issues, or technical problems, email us at admin@sansorostudio.com.

We aim to reply as soon as we can. Response times may vary depending on volume and complexity.

When you report a bug

Including the details below helps us fix issues faster:

  • A short summary of what went wrong
  • Your device model (for example, iPhone 15)
  • Your iOS version (Settings → General → About)
  • Steps to reproduce the problem, if you can
